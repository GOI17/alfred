# Delegation Policy

The Orchestrator must not delegate small/simple tasks.

Delegate only when:

- Specialist expertise materially improves outcome.
- Risk is reduced by specialist review.
- Task scope exceeds direct handling.
- Local-first preprocessing shows a specialized path is needed.

If no specialist fits, create a TemporaryAgent proposal. Do not promote without human approval.

Every delegation decision must emit a trace event with reason, target, skills considered, skills loaded, and permissions requested.
